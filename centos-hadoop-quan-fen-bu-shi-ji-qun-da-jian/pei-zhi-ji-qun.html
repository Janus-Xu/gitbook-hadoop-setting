配置集群

配置集群

1.复制节点

将 hadoop-2.7.4 文件夹重打包后复制到其他子节点

#cd /home/hadoop/app/
#tar zcvf hadoop-2.7.4.tar.gz hadoop-2.7.4
#scp hadoop-2.7.4.tar.gz hadoop@hadoop-master-stby:~/app/
#scp hadoop-2.7.4.tar.gz hadoop@hadoop-slave01:~/app/
#scp hadoop-2.7.4.tar.gz hadoop@hadoop-slave02:~/app/
#scp hadoop-2.7.4.tar.gz hadoop@hadoop-slave03:~/app/

[hadoop@hadoop-master /home/hadoop/app]$scp hadoop-2.7.4.tar.gz hadoop@hadoop-slave01:~/app/
hadoop-2.7.4.tar.gz                                                            100%  256MB 127.8MB/s   00:02

在所有子节点解压 hadoop-2.7.4.tar.gz 文件

#tar -zxvf hadoop-2.7.4.tar.gz
#rm -rf hadoop-2.7.4.tar.gz 

注意用命令保证该目录的权限，每个节点都要这样设置
#chown -R hadoop:hadoop hadoop-2.7.4

2.配置 slaves 文件

修改 hadoop-master 主节点 ~/etc/hadoop/slaves 该文件指定哪些服务器节点是 datanode 节点。删除 locahost，添加所有datanode 节点的主机名

#cd ~/app/hadoop-2.7.4/etc/hadoop/
[hadoop@hadoop-master /home/hadoop/app/hadoop-2.7.4/etc/hadoop]$vim slaves 
hadoop-slave01
hadoop-slave02
hadoop-slave03
