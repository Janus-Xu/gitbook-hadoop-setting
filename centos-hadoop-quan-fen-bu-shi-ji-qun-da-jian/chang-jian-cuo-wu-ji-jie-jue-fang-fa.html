2.Hadoop 启动的时候 DataNode 报错 Problem connecting to server: hadoop-master/10.181.0.11:9000

错误日志
2017-10-22 09:34:23,808 INFO org.apache.hadoop.ipc.Client: Retrying connect to server: hadoop-master/10.181.0.11:9000. Already tried 6 time(s); retry policy is RetryUpToMaximumCountWithFixedSleep(maxRetries=10, sleepTime=1000 MILLISECONDS)
2017-10-22 09:34:24,808 INFO org.apache.hadoop.ipc.Client: Retrying connect to server: hadoop-master/10.181.0.11:9000. Already tried 7 time(s); retry policy is RetryUpToMaximumCountWithFixedSleep(maxRetries=10, sleepTime=1000 MILLISECONDS)
2017-10-22 09:34:25,809 INFO org.apache.hadoop.ipc.Client: Retrying connect to server: hadoop-master/10.181.0.11:9000. Already tried 8 time(s); retry policy is RetryUpToMaximumCountWithFixedSleep(maxRetries=10, sleepTime=1000 MILLISECONDS)
2017-10-22 09:34:26,809 INFO org.apache.hadoop.ipc.Client: Retrying connect to server: hadoop-master/10.181.0.11:9000. Already tried 9 time(s); retry policy is RetryUpToMaximumCountWithFixedSleep(maxRetries=10, sleepTime=1000 MILLISECONDS)

如果你已经检查了 datanode 以及 namenode 的连通性（比如防火墙），IP 都正确，但是仍然没法解决问题，那么其实根本原因还是无法连接到10.181.0.11:9000 相应ip的相应端口

查看一下 namenode 的端口情况：

[hadoop@hadoop-master /home/hadoop/app/hadoop-2.7.4/sbin]$netstat -an | grep 9000
tcp        0      0 127.0.0.1:9000          0.0.0.0:*               LISTEN

其实问题就再这里，hadoop 启动的时候只监听了 127.0.0.1 一个端口，而不是 10.181.0.11:9000。这种情况下，也就只有本机才能联通 127.0.0.1:9000 了。看来是 IP 解析的问题，看一下 namenode 的 hosts 文件：

[hadoop@hadoop-master /home/hadoop/app/hadoop-2.7.4/sbin]$ cat /etc/hosts
10.181.0.11 hadoop-master
10.181.9.43 hadoop-slave01
10.181.10.145 hadoop-slave02
10.181.10.72 hadoop-slave03
127.0.0.1   localhost  localhost.localdomain  hadoop-master
::1         localhost localhost.localdomain localhost6 localhost6.localdomain6

主要问题在于 127.0.0.1 的 hadoop-master ，只要将 hadoop-master 去掉即可。
